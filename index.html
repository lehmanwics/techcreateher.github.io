    About teachCreateHER

    tech createHER challenge is a hackathon, renamed as a tech challenge to be more inviting of women at all skill levels. The challenge will bring together women from diverse educational and technical backgrounds to create, innovate, and collaborate on technical solutions to real-world problems. We are creating a community that will help close the gap of women leaders in the tech industry and encourage more women to seek careers in tech.

    Join us!

    FAQ

    • Do I have to be a woman to attend?

      Lehman College Women in Computer Science (WiCS) is looking to build a community where marginalized people in tech like women can feel welcomed and ready to take on an exciting challenge. To accomplish this, we need all hands on deck! As such, we do not require identification as any specific gender to attend but do encourage you to leave an opportunity open to those who may not have had this opportunity before.

    • Is this an overnight event?

      Nope! We are hosting a single-day event this year on Friday, May 10th, 2019 and look forward to seeing you there and at future events.

    • Who is hosting this?

      The Lehman College Women in Computer Science (WiCS) is bringing this event to Lehman College and CUNY at large. Check us out online!
