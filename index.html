    About teachCreateHER

    tech createHer is a hackathon, renamed as a “tech challenge” to be more inviting of women at all skill levels. The challenge will bring together women from diverse educational and technical backgrounds to create, innovate, and collaborate on technical solutions to real-world problems. We are creating a community that will help close the gap of women leaders in the tech industry and encourage more women to seek careers in tech.
